About Shloka

Overview

Shloka is an interactive platform designed to make the teachings of the Bhagavad Gita accessible and engaging. Featuring a chatbot and multilingual interface, users can explore shlokas, listen to recitations, and understand their profound meanings.

Key Features

  • Shloka Exploration with translations in Telugu, English, and Hindi.
  • Audio Support for listening to shloka recitations.
  • Summarization in multiple languages.
  • AI-powered Chatbot for Bhagavad Gita Q&A.
  • Random Image Generation inspired by Gita themes.
  • Firebase Integration for real-time data management.

Objective

The project blends ancient wisdom with modern technology to create a bridge between traditional spiritual teachings and contemporary learning tools, making the Bhagavad Gita accessible to all.

Target Audience

Perfect for students, educators, and anyone interested in exploring the spiritual and philosophical depth of the Bhagavad Gita.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Backend: Firebase for database integration
  • Audio and Visual Support: Real-time audio streaming and interactive graphics
  • AI: Chatbot powered by a custom dataset of Gita-related Q&A
